# Privacy Policy for Yet Another Site Blocker

Last Updated: 08th of May 2025

Thank you for using Yet Another Site Blocker ("the Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information We Do Not Collect

Yet Another Site Blocker is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, browsing history (other than the URLs you explicitly provide for blocking), or any other sensitive personal data.

## Information You Provide for Blocking

- **Websites to Block:** You provide specific website URLs that you wish to block.
- **Time Schedules:** You may provide start and end times for scheduled blocking.
- **Local Block Statistics:** The Extension can keep local counts of blocked navigation attempts per domain so you can review your own blocking history.

## How This Information is Used and Stored

- **Local Storage:** All website URLs, time schedules, and optional block statistics are stored exclusively locally on your computer using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API.
- **Purpose:** This information is used solely by the Extension to perform its core function: blocking access to the websites you have specified, according to the schedules you have set, and showing you local blocked-navigation counts.
- **No External Transmission:** Your configured blocklist, schedules, and local statistics are NEVER transmitted to any external servers or third parties. All processing happens locally within your browser.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security is tied to the security of your computer and your Chrome browser profile.

## Data Removal

You can remove your configured data at any time by:

- Deleting individual rules within the Extension's options page.
- Resetting the locally stored statistics from the Extension's options page.
- Uninstalling the Yet Another Site Blocker extension, which will remove all locally stored data associated with it.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the extension's store listing or options page.
